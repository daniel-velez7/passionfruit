My Journey Into Wellness

During my childhood, I began to experience chronic fatigue. After some trial & error, I discovered acupuncture - an Eastern modality that provided support Western medicine could not provide. I was cared for emotionally as well as physically and because of the intimate sessions I received, I began to deeply admire the work. As a result, I realized that I wanted to facilitate healing through this hands-on modality. So, I relocated to China in pursuit of a fully immersive educational experience. To obtain my Master’s Degree, I provided acupuncture in Chinese hospitals and wrote an original research paper that has since been published in multiple medical journals. Although acupuncture helped, the fatigue persisted and when Covid catapulted through the world, my health rippled backwards. During this timeframe, I received a Quantum Krystic Healing session. Immediately, I felt an internal shift and as a result of this one session, my entire future trajectory changed. Instead of seeing the set backs, I now saw a world of possibilities at my fingertips.

Through Quantum Krystic Healing, I have had the opportunity to cultivate an understanding of my internal beliefs and their effect on me. For example: how the residual trauma of my chronic illness repeatedly sabotaged my attempts at healing. By dismantling the idea that sympathy equated love, I simultaneously grasped how I craved disempowerment because it felt safe and familiar. In fact, I initially wrote about my journey into wellness from a state of mind clouded by victimhood. Afterall, if sympathy equated love then I needed to stay pitiable. Through Quantum Krystic Healing, I have recognised this pattern and my responsibility in creating my experience of myself. Although I still experience health dysregulation, the duration only shortens & the frequency lessens. It’s worth noting how my change in perception has impacted my experience of chronic fatigue: now, I greet these moments with compassion & acceptance rather than a sense of personal failure or fear. Even emotional dysregulation is seen as an opportunity to notice what disempowering beliefs I still hold onto and how I can adjust my beliefs accordingly. I hope that by sharing my story, you feel empowered to travel deeper into yourself - to discern the blocks creating resistance towards living your highest path.
